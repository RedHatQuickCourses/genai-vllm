Lab Environment

Instructions to Launch Your Lab on the Red Hat Online Learning (ROLE) Platform

  1. Log in to the ROLE platform

  2. Search for the catalog entry: FIXME Lab Catalog Name.

  3. Click on the Launch button for the catalog entry in the search results.

  4. On the Course page, click the Lab Environment tab.

  5. Click on the Create button on the Lab Environment page to launch the lab.

  6. Wait for the lab creation process to complete.

  7. When the lab is created, refer to the SSH Private Key & Instructions on the page to access your lab.

ROLE Portal Links

  • RedHat associates: https://role.rhu.redhat.com/rol-rhu/app/

  • RedHat partners: https://rol.redhat.com/rol/app

Red Hat Developer Platform

Instructions to Launch Your Lab on the Red Hat Developer Platform

  1. Log in Red Hat Developer Sandbox

  2. From from product menu dropdown entry: Select Red Hat OpenShift AI.

  3. Click on the Try it our no cost developer sandbox button, once logged in, you will be taken the Red Hat Hybird Cloud Console dashboard.

  4. On the page, scroll down in the menu then under the OpenShift AI, select the Developer Sandbox | OpenShift AI option.

  5. Click on the Get Started button on the Start exploring Developer Sandbox launch the lab.

  6. Wait for the lab creation process to complete.

  7. When the lab is created, refer to the SSH Private Key & Instructions on the page to access your lab.

There is no GPU provided with this environment, however you can select to deploy a model that runs on CPU. This is great solution for instantly ready lab environment to create a workbench, deploy an notebook, interact with remote model, develop and test scripts and notebooks, and perform any of OpenShift AI tasks that do not require a GPU, like design pipelines. Easy and Fast environment, but no control of the backend services, or admin access to OpenShift or OpenShift AI.
